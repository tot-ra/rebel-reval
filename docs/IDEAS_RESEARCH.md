# Ideas & Research Plans

This document tracks brainstorming concepts, narrative ideas, and mechanics that require further research and evaluation before being added to the official canon or active development roadmap.

## Narrative & Lore
- **Introductory Scene:** Create a cinematic scene with the Danish King piercing a tree, declaring he will bring order among the witches.
- **Thematic Core:** The game is fundamentally about a "battle of values".
  - **Rulers vs. Citizens:** Establish the ruling class as distinct from normal citizens.
  - **Livonian Order:** Represents knighthood, military order, romance, and honor.
- **Blacksmith Loop:** Incorporate a daytime routine where a blacksmith completes forging commissions for important nobles.
- **Nobles:** Ensure each noble has their own distinct flaws.
- **Characters:** Introduce "Daro", the jailer.

## Gameplay Mechanics
- **Shadows & Lighting:** Implement Nox-style shadows and line-of-sight.
- **Environment:** Add interactable barrels, secret rooms, and destructible walls.
- **Combat Adjustments:**
  - Introduce archer enemies.
  - Add round shields.
  - Implement fast sword attack animations.
- **World Events:** Consider adding public executions.

## Locations & Worldbuilding

### Novgorod
- **Quests:** Add a quest centered around Novgorod birch bark manuscripts.
- **Authentic Names:** Use historically accurate names like Onfim, Zhiznomir, Mikula, Yermola, Grigory, Ozekey, Oksinya, Onaniy, Rodivon, Ignatko, Ivanka, Martyn, Khrap, Savliy, Maksim, Prokosha, Netsayev, Sidor, Taduy, Ladopga, Grishka, Kostoy, Selivestr, Lunka, Aluya, Zakhariy, Mikhey.
- **Locations:** Include or reference nearby cities like Staraya Russa and Koporye.
- **Linguistics:** Implement the Novgorod dialect (e.g., substituting "ч" with "ц").
- **Economy:** Introduce a trade/slave-trade dynamic between Pskov and Novgorod.

## Player Archetypes & Mechanics (Bartle Taxonomy)
Ensure the game loop caters to the 4 main gamer types:
- **Achievers:** Focus on metrics and XP scaling.
- **Explorers:** Focus on locations, secret doors, and interactive environments.
- **Killers:** Focus on sorting loot, building high DPS/defense, and surviving enemy waves (e.g., against the Order/Black cloaks).
- **Socializers:** Focus on quests, talking to NPCs (clerics), and building structures (masonry).
