# Mart (The Apprentice)

**Confidence label:** `invented`
**Role:** Inciting incident & Emotional stake

## Motivations & Core
- **Want:** Prove he is not a child and turn the forge into a weapon for Estonian liberation.
- **Fear:** That Kalev's caution is actually cowardice, and that waiting for the "right time" will outlive every chance for real change.
- **Contradiction:** He passionately fights for freedom and the oppressed, but is willing to use the same ruthless, catastrophic violence as their oppressors to achieve it.
- **Secret or withheld fact:** He secretly passed rejected spearheads bearing Kalev's maker mark to the Black Cloaks (the rebels).

## Relationships
- **Kalev:** Master of the forge. Mart seeks his approval but is deeply frustrated by his passive neutrality.
- **Aita:** Aunt-figure who patches up his scrapes and tries to keep him grounded in reality.
- **Kaja:** Idealized rebel contact; Mart sees her as a true hero of the cause.
- **Captain Henning:** The embodiment of foreign oppression; Mart hates him completely.

## Voice
Passionate, impatient, and certain of right and wrong. Speaks quickly and emotionally, often naive but fiercely courageous.

## Possible outcomes
- Disciplined organizer.
- Frightened informer.
- Dead martyr.
- Survivor who completely rejects Kalev's worldview.
