# Kaja (The Bilingual Courier)

**Confidence label:** `invented`
**Role:** Rebellion liaison & Strategic manipulator

## Motivations & Core
- **Want:** Build a coalition broad enough to prevent an indiscriminate slaughter of Estonians by the ruling class.
- **Fear:** That each side will treat her mixed background (or her ability to navigate both worlds) as inherent proof of betrayal.
- **Contradiction:** She manipulates people for a genuinely humane goal, frequently withholding information that could save individuals in order to protect the wider network.
- **Secret or withheld fact:** She knows the rural uprising is launching prematurely and is doomed to fail unless the city gates are sabotaged from within, but she lies to her city contacts about their odds of success.

## Relationships
- **Kalev:** A crucial, highly useful asset. She tries to recruit him by appealing to his conscience and his craft.
- **Mart:** A useful but dangerously reckless tool. She uses his enthusiasm but keeps him at arm's length from true operational secrets.
- **Captain Henning:** A dangerous adversary; she must constantly avoid his patrols.

## Voice
Articulate, adaptable, and calm under intense pressure. She code-switches seamlessly between high Low German and native Estonian depending on her audience, always saying exactly what the listener needs to hear.

## Possible outcomes
- Trusted organizer who saves many lives.
- Ruthless operator who sacrifices allies for the cause.
- Exposed fugitive forced to flee Reval.
