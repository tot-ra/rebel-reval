# Captain Henning (Commander of the Viru Watch)

**Confidence label:** `plausible composite`
**Role:** Recurring antagonist & Legitimate argument for order

## Motivations & Core
- **Want:** Prevent a massacre in the city and preserve the authority of the Watch, which gives him the means to maintain order.
- **Fear:** That the Livonian Order will intervene and replace the city watch with military occupation if he appears weak or loses control.
- **Contradiction:** He is capable of showing surprising individual mercy face-to-face, while systematically building a machine of collective punishment for the districts.
- **Secret or withheld fact:** He knows his own watchmen are corrupt, hoarding supplies, and instigating violence, but he covers it up because he cannot afford a mutiny on the eve of an uprising.

## Relationships
- **Kalev:** Respects Kalev's skill and relies on him for Watch equipment, but deeply suspects his loyalties.
- **Mart:** Views him as a radicalized delinquent who needs to be made an example of.
- **Kaja:** Suspects her as a courier and actively hunts her network.
- **Jürgen Witte:** Dislikes his profiteering but is forced to protect his warehouses to keep the city funded.

## Voice
Exhausted authority. Procedural, measured, and weary, but capable of sudden, decisive violence. He speaks like a man carrying a heavy structure on his shoulders.

## Possible outcomes
- Exposed for his corruption and loses command.
- Commits an atrocity in the name of order.
- Executes a restrained final response, minimizing casualties.
- Successfully seals the city and hands it over to the Order.
