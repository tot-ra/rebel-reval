# Jürgen Witte (Hanseatic Amber Merchant)

**Confidence label:** `plausible composite`
**Role:** Economic temptation & Material supplier

## Motivations & Core
- **Want:** Keep the harbor open and convert political instability into personal control of debt and city supply.
- **Fear:** That open war will make contracts meaningless, empowering armed men (like Henning or the Order) over merchants and wealth.
- **Contradiction:** He prevents shortages when relief is profitable, but actively manufactures scarcity (hoarding grain/water) when it drives up his leverage.
- **Secret or withheld fact:** He has already negotiated a secret contingency contract with the Livonian Order to exclusively supply their armies if they successfully besiege and take over Reval, hedging his bets against the Danish Crown.

## Relationships
- **Kalev:** Sees Kalev as a useful contractor and pawn. Offers him safety and iron in exchange for dirty work.
- **Aita:** A minor competitor and client. He attempts to squeeze her brewery out of business during the water crisis.
- **Captain Henning:** An obstacle to free trade. Jürgen uses bribery and political leverage to bypass Henning's blockades.

## Voice
Suave, transactional, polite but quietly menacing. He speaks about human lives, starvation, and war purely in terms of investments, risks, and yields.

## Possible outcomes
- Becomes a massive profiteer during the siege.
- Bankrupt and exiled if his warehouses are breached and his contracts exposed.
- Instrumental in saving the city's supply if forced to open his vaults.
